Senior House has had a long, colorful history. But don't let saggy old people tell you it was better back in the day... they're just bitter.

People have taken a number of random stabs at compiling mural galleries and writen histories, so that our great, great, starved and emaciated grandchildren can look back and see the sad lives we lived during college while we still had running water and a breathable atmosphere.

Because we know everybody wants to hear about our college escapades, here are a few of the motley attempts at reliving the magic...

Sport Death: the wickid hahdcore motto

"sport death" is the de facto motto of Senior House, along with "only life can kill you". It came from a Hunter S. Thompson novel. "that's about all there is to it"
