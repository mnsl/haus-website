in case of emergency

Call MIT's emergency hotline at 617-253-1212 if someone is in urgent need of medical attention.

If the situation is less urgent or you are not sure what to do, call Student Security and we will be there to help right away.

Never hesitate to call for help if someone you know is in need of medical attention.

Under MIT's Good Samaritan Amnesty Policy, a student who is transported due to an alcohol or prohibited substance related emergency will not face disciplinary action.

haus & courtyard access

All members of the MIT community are welcome in the courtyard to see bands, mud wrestle, and partake in various other festivities. Just bring your MIT ID.

If you're an alum, you will need to register for courtyard and daytime house access.

You need to be registered as a resident's guest to be indoors after 1am.

policy on substance use

Drug use and overdrinking will not be tolerated. Student Security can and will ask you to leave.

The Muddy will be serving beer and wine to 21+ attendees in the courtyard, but alcohol consumption is not permitted in the lobby and other common areas inside haus.

The haus community has worked hard this year to demonstrate our commitment to health and safety. We expect you to make safe choices.
