Statement Regarding Steer Roast

"Due to an ongoing review of credible information regarding dangerous behavior that occurred in Senior House during the turnaround, the administration has placed the Senior House community on probation and cancelled Steer Roast. The Division of Student Life (DSL) and Steer Roast veeps are currently working together on the cancellation, and DSL will cover out of pocket costs the House has incurred planning the event.

The administration and Senior House residents understand that this news will disappoint individuals who registered to attend next month. However, given the seriousness of this situation and our collective responsibility to ensure the community's safety and wellbeing, we ask that you respect the decision and give us the time we need to handle this matter privately and responsibly."

- Office of the Chancellor, Steer Roast veeps, and Senior House presidents

Comments can be sent to steer-roast@mit.edu.
